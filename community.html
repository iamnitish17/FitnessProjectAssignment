WE TAKE YOUR WORKOUT PERSONALLY


Your health is your own our job is to help you own it. The fitness and health club industry is always touting the latest and greatest fitness trends, but the best workout is the one you love and that loves you back. At Community Fitness in Seattle, we take our role as coach and cheerleader seriously. Whether you find motivation through high-energy in-person classes in our neighborhood gym, or you prefer online workouts at your own pace, we are committed to offering a variety of classes, formats and ways to support your journey to optimal health.


You deserve instructors that you can relate to and who know how to personalize each workout to your specific goals. Our studio finds the best fitness professionals and gives you lots of ways to connect with them in-person or virtually. Our world-class coaches learn your name, your style and your goals to personalize and customize what fitness means to you. Our priority is to design and offer group exercise programs at all levels and abilities so that health is accessible for everyone.


Our fitness instructors take your workout personally! Their passion inspires grit and determination. Join them in-person at our Seattle gym, livestream their weekly classes or access all their past workouts On Demand… and find out who motivates you the most. Fitness trends come and go, but relationships can last a lifetime.
